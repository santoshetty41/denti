Dental Implants

Dental implants are artificial tooth roots that are surgically placed into the jawbone to support a replacement tooth or bridge. They are made of titanium or a titanium alloy, which is biocompatible and can fuse with the surrounding bone tissue, creating a strong and durable foundation for the replacement teeth.
